Sagittarius


Characteristics

Element: Fire

Cardinality: Mutable

Positive: Wise/Creative

Negative: Too moralistic/Fickle

Planet: Jupiter

Traits

Description: Adventurous

Positive: Flexible, adaptable, loves life and liberty, open, extraverted, sincere kindness, creative, imaginative

Negative: Direct, irresponsible, lack of continuity, does not end what is started, lack of discipline, inconsistent
